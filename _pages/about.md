I am an Assistant Professor of Accounting at the [Haas School of Business](https://haas.berkeley.edu), University of California, Berkeley. My research focuses on the effectiveness of disclosure regulation and standard-setting, with particular emphasis on financial institutions and the role of information in capital markets.

Prior to joining Berkeley Haas, I served as a Post-Doctoral Fellow at the [Financial Accounting Standards Board](https://www.fasb.org) from 2021–2022.
