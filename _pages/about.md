Tanya Paul is an Assistant Professor of Accounting at the [Haas School of Business](https://haas.berkeley.edu), University of California, Berkeley. Her research focuses on the effectiveness of disclosure regulation and standard-setting, with particular emphasis on financial institutions and the role of information in capital markets.

Prior to joining Berkeley Haas, she served as a Post-Doctoral Fellow at the [Financial Accounting Standards Board](https://www.fasb.org) from 2021–2022. She holds a Ph.D. in Accounting and a B.S. in Economics from [The Wharton School](https://www.wharton.upenn.edu) at the University of Pennsylvania.
